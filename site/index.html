How it works

Capture the work. Keelson writes the record.

Your crew already takes the photos and knows what they found. Keelson does the part they hate — turning it into the document your customer will accept.

01 — CAPTURE

From the deck

Phone photos and a few dictated notes — frame stations, UT readings, what the crew found. Rough is fine.

02 — GENERATE

Audit-ready draft

Keelson drafts the document in the format your contract requires — every line traceable back to the photo and note it came from. Nothing invented.

03 — VERIFY & SIGN

Human sign-off

Your QA lead checks each section against the evidence and signs off — then exports a submission-ready package in the format your customer's system expects. The expert verifies instead of writing from scratch.

Security & deployment

Built for controlled information from day one.

Most software can't legally touch this work. Keelson is designed so the controlled information never leaves the environment you already trust — which is why adoption doesn't require a security exception.

Your CUI never leaves your boundary

Keelson deploys as software inside your environment. You remain the custodian of all controlled information — we never store, hold, or move it.

The AI runs inside the boundary too

No prompts, photos, or notes are sent to outside clouds. Inference runs within your environment — on-prem or your authorized GovCloud — so nothing escapes.

Supports your CMMC boundary

Designed to sit cleanly inside a Level 2 / NIST SP 800-171 scope with a clear customer responsibility matrix. Keelson is a tool, not a certification.

Every line traces to its evidence

Each statement links back to the photo and field note behind it. Auditable end to end — the opposite of a black box.
